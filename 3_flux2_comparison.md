# HilbertA on Flux.2-klein 9B vs. other methods

Here we show comparisons of 24 sets of outputs generated from identical inputs with Flux.2-klein 9B and various inference-time methods. For each group, columns correspond to (from left to right):  
1. FLUX.2-klein 9B (input)  
2. Hilberta / 4 Tiles  
3. Hilberta / 16 Tiles  
4. Sparge Attention  
5. CLEAR / r=8  
6. CLEAR / r=16

Hilberta exhibits consistent high quality across models, comparing to other methods.
