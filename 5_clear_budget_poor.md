# CLEAR Needs More Data: Poor Quality Under HilbertA Training Data Budget

Using the same training data as HilbertA (LoRA, 40k images), CLEAR yields poor results. As noted, CLEAR requires full finetuning on attention and at least 750k images to achieve acceptable quality—significantly more than the data allocated for HilbertA.
